How It Works

The app uses a CNN model to analyze facial expressions and classify them into 7 emotion categories. It then visualizes the activation maps of different layers in the network to show which parts of the image are being focused on by the model.

Understanding Activation Maps

Activation maps highlight regions of the input image that activate specific filters in the CNN. Warmer colors (red/yellow) indicate stronger activations, showing which parts of the face are most important for detecting specific emotions.
